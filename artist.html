Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is an American singer and songwriter. She first gained public attention in 2015 with her debut single "Ocean Eyes", which was subsequently released by Darkroom, an imprint of Interscope Records. Eilish has received several accolades, including multiple Grammy Awards, and is known for her distinctive voice and unique fashion style.

Central Cee

Central Cee is a British rapper and songwriter from Shepherd's Bush, London. He rose to prominence with the release of his single "Loading" in 2020. Known for his distinctive style and introspective lyrics, Central Cee has quickly become a prominent figure in the UK rap scene.
